## // who i am

I'm a cybersecurity analyst and ethical hacker based in Kenya. I specialize in penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and building defensive security systems. I'm passionate about CTF challenges and open-source security tooling.

I'm currently open to **red team**, **blue team**, and **SOC analyst** roles.

---

## // what i do

- **Penetration Testing** — web apps, networks, and APIs using industry-standard tools and methodologies (OWASP, PTES, NIST)
- **Vulnerability Assessment** — identifying and reporting security weaknesses in systems before attackers do
- **SIEM & Log Analysis** — building dashboards and alert rules in ELK Stack and Splunk
- **CTF Competitions** — active player on HackTheBox and TryHackMe, focusing on web, crypto, and forensics
